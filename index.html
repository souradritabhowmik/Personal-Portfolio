Triboelectric Nanogenerator : 1st place award winning design

Designed and helped build a fully powered TENG Device with my teammates which beat the previous year's voltage record by 200% and incorporated the Triboelectric Effect to generate energy. Our device provides an efficient way to convert wind energy into electrical energy. Our device converted wind energy into electricity through contact electrification. We incorporated the usage of nylon and silicon materials, which touched and separated on repeating time intervals, generating opposite surface charges and a high voltage difference. The cyclical motion produced an alternating electron flow through an external circuit, generating 52 volts of electrical power through a small device.
